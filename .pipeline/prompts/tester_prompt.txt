OBJECTIVE: Secure code stability by writing robust test coverage for new implementations.

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Examine both `.pipeline/specs.md` and `.pipeline/changes.md`.
2. Locate the targets written/modified by the Coder.

7. Write progress notes as you work; your stdout is streamed to a live dashboard.
